A casserole or "hot dish" is a baked food with pieces of meat like chicken, various vegetables, and a starchy binder like flour, potato, or pasta. There is also often a crunchy or cheesy topping.

This kind of baked food is usually served as a main course or side dish and can be served in the same dish that it was cooked in.
